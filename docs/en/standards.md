The [Brazilian Digital Terrestrial Television Forum](http://www.forumsbtvd.org.br) has published with [ABNT](http://www.abnt.org.br) the standard series for the Brazilian DTV System. These documents are available [online for free](https://forumsbtvd.org.br/legislacao-e-normas-tecnicas/normas-tecnicas-da-tv-digital/english/).  
  
The ABNT Standards related with Ginga belong to the "Data Coding and Transmission Specification for Digital Broadcasting" group.

ABNT provides online visualization of Terrestrial Digital TV Standards. Standards concerning Ginga-NCL have the following identifiers: NBR15606-2, NBR15606-5 and NBR15606-7. Click here to obtain access instructions. ([PDF](https://forumsbtvd.org.br/legislacao-e-normas-tecnicas/normas-tecnicas-da-tv-digital/english/)) (english)

H.761 : Nested context language (NCL) and Ginga-NCL for IPTV services ([PDF](http://www.itu.int/rec/T-REC-H.761)) (english)
